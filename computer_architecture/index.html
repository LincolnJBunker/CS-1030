Commputer Architecture

Interview and Scenario

The person that I interviewed was my brother. He is a Software Engineer. He has experience working as a Full Stack Software Engineer, but in recent years has transitioned to mainly work on backend development. I asked him about some of the specs that he'd want his computer to have, and he broke down each of the following for me

  • CPU: plenty of cores and threads for running docker, local DBs and possible VMs.
  • RAM: at least 32 GB if budget allows, if not 16 GB
  • Memory: at least 1TB or close
  • Ports / I/O: good cooling so it doesn't overheat/throttle under heavy loads
  • OS: prefers Mac OS, but isn't opposed to Windows
  • Battery & Portability: be able to go 8 - 10 hours without a charge, laptop preferred

After getting some of his feedback and looking into some options, here are the 3 computers that I think would be a good fit for him:

  • Apple MackBook Pro (M4)
  • ASUS Zenbook 14 OLED 32GB / 1TB
  • ASUS ProArt P16 OLED Ryzen AI-9 HX

In conclusion, based on all of the different factors I feel like my brother should go with the MacBook Pro. I think that if he were to upgrade from the 'base' model and get one with 32 GB of RAM and 1TB of Storage, it would allow him to do everything that he needs to. He is familiar with the macOS operating system. He personally likes the UI and ease of use that comes with an Apple product
