What is Trad Climbing?

Trad climbing is similar to sport climbing. However, instead of having bolts in the wall, you have to place your own gear, called cams, nuts, or hexes, into the wall yourself. You place them in cracks in the wall. Trad is short for 'Traditional climbing'. It is how all climbing was done when the sport began. An appeal to trad climbing is you are a lot more free to climb whatever you want, and you can climb longer routes too.

What Gear will you Need?

With trad climbing, you will want all the gear needed for sport climbing. You will also need:

  • Cams
  • Hexes
  • Nuts
  • Lots of extra carabiners
  • Slings, webbing
